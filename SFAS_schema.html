05 Business Modules
🛒
Sales & AR
Manages the full order-to-cash cycle. Sales orders trigger automatic GL postings through the event bus via posting rules, eliminating manual entry.
  • Sales Orders
  • Invoicing
  • AR Aging
  • Credit Control
  • Revenue Recognition
🔧
Procurement & AP
Purchase order lifecycle from requisition through 3-way matching. Auto-posts AP entries when goods receipts and invoices are matched.
  • Purchase Orders
  • GRN Matching
  • AP Aging
  • Vendor Payments
  • Materials Planning
📦
Inventory
Multi-method costing engine supporting FIFO, LIFO, and Weighted Average. All stock movements auto-generate COGS and inventory GL entries.
  • FIFO / LIFO / AVG
  • Stock Transfers
  • Stock Valuation
  • Reorder Alerts
  • Write-offs
👥
HR & Payroll
Full payroll processing with tax computation, deduction management, and auto-posting to salary expense and liability accounts per period.
  • Employee Master
  • Payroll Engine
  • Tax Withholding
  • Time & Attendance
  • Expense Claims
🏗️
Fixed Assets
Asset lifecycle management with automated depreciation posting. Supports SLM, DDB, SYD methods. Generates depreciation schedules and disposal entries.
  • Asset Register
  • Auto Depreciation
  • Disposals
  • Revaluation
  • Impairment
🏦
Banking & Cash
Multi-bank, multi-currency account management with automated bank reconciliation. Matches bank statement lines to GL entries using AI-assisted matching.
  • Bank Reconciliation
  • Multi-Currency
  • Payments
  • Receipts
  • Cash Pooling
💼
Budget Control
Real-time budget vs actual monitoring with variance alerts. Supports annual, revised, and rolling budgets at account and cost center level.

  • Variance Analysis
  • Rolling Forecast
  • AI Prediction

General Ledger
The single source of financial truth. Consolidates all module postings, maintains period balances, handles intercompany eliminations and multi-currency translation.

Manufacturing & BOM
